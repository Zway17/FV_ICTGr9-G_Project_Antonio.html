Fires are dangerous phenomenons. They can happen when you least expect it, they can even happen in the smallest of conditions. But sometimes, one spark is enough to start a raging fire. As we know, Fire is a destructive force that can be controlled, but if let untouched can be disasterous.
Despite that, you can easily control fires through these easy steps.





HOW TO PREVENT A FIRE
Don't smoke in the house If you smoke, or a family member, it is important to remember to smoke in well ventilated areas and you should make sure that the cigarette you have smoked has been completely extinguished. As known, the Cigarette must be lit on fire to be enjoyed by people. Thus, an unproperly snuffed cigar can lead to a spread of fire.
Keep Flamables away from electrical sockets Any flamable pieces of trash or material should be kept away from electrical sockets, or any form of heat conductor that involves flame. If you happen to misplace certain objects in your household, that could turn into somethning hard to control.
Unplug Appliances that are not being used. We all know electrical devices generate heat when they do actions. But lets say you forget to unplug an electrical appliance to its source, well, it can over heat overtime to the point they can cause electrical fires! Not only that, you could also be spending much more on that electrical bill. Either way, remember to unplug your appliances when done using them.
